This Is Jerome Neil, The Man With A Story

1993 North Central Bronx Hospital, Jerome Neil was born into a family. The father figure wasn't around for Jerome. His mother (Darlene) was working back breaking doubles sometimes tripples at the local diner, so she can afford food.

Before Jerome knew it, he was homeless in the southern part of the Bronx, Gang Territory. He was forced to fend for himself.
